WHO WE ARE

A LABATORY

ALAB is an interactive media art studio established by media artists who majored in engineering, design and video. We use various media technologies to cross the boundaries of technology and art, providing the audience with immersive experiences.


In an attempt to create experiences that have never existed at the intersection of technology and art, we constantly carry out research and express them through art. ALAB.‘s works bring interesting experiences via the uniqueness that has never existed before by realizing our imaginations with technology.
